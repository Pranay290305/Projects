 SMART WHEEL CHAIR

 Smart wheelchair using Arduino Uno With Bluetooth and  voice control, implementing obstacle avoidance, safety monitoring, and sensor integration, 
 and enabling IoT-based remote tracking and cloud data logging for enhanced accessibility and caregiver support.
