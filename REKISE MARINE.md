# Projects

REKISE MARINE

Designed power-system PCBs for AUVs powering all onboard sensors and subsystems. Worked with altimeter and DVL sensors for Jalkapi XLUUV. Developed and bench-tested BMS cell balancing for Swadheen autonomous surface platform.Designed PCBs and sensor integration (DVL,ALTIMETER) modules for AUV& XLUUV systems, supporting embedded systems and electronics for endurance platforms.
Contributed to the development of an advanced Battery Management System (BMS) for long-duration naval trials, enhancing energy systems, diagnostics
Supporting projects for the Indian Navy, Goa Shipyard Limited (GSL), and Garden Reach Shipbuilders & Engineers (GRSE) with focus on system integration, testing, and power electronics
